Pyramid Tool
Infos:
  • Cooldown: 2,5s
  • Maximum. messages in a row: 25
  • Check if you are Moderator or Vip in this channel.
Rate limits:
  • Docs: Rate-limits
  • If the user isn’t the channel’s broadcaster or moderator, the bot may send a maximum of 20 messages per 30 seconds.
  • If the user is the channel’s broadcaster or moderator, the bot may send a maximum of 100 messages per 30 seconds.
  • The bot is limited to sending 7,500 messages per 30 seconds across all channels. This means that the bot could send 10 messages per 30 seconds to 750 different channels. However, it couldn’t, for example, send 7,500 messages per 30 seconds to a single channel because it would exceed the 20 messages per 30 second limit.
